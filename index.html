About Lance

I am a proud member of the Campbell County community and a firm believer that public service means serving the people first. As County Clerk, I will work hard every day, operate with honesty, and be transparent in how the office serves our citizens.

My Priorities

Citizens First

Making the citizens of Campbell County the number one priority. Every decision should serve the public and improve their experience with local government.

Trust in Local Government

Trust is earned. I am committed to honesty and transparency so citizens can have confidence in their County Clerk’s Office.

Efficiency & Dependability

The Clerk’s Office should be efficient, organized, and dependable. I will focus on improving processes and delivering accurate, timely service.
